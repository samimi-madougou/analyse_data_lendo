E-Commerce Sales Analytics | DataLendo Case Study
📊 Project Overview
This project simulates a real-world mission for DataLendo, an electronics retailer (Smartphones, Laptops, TV) operating across French-speaking markets. As a Junior Data Analyst, my mission was to clean, process, and analyze sales data to provide the management team with a 360-degree view of their commercial performance.

This repository demonstrates expertise in:

End-to-End Data Analysis (Cleaning to Insights)

Retail KPIs Calculation (AOV, Revenue Growth, RFM)

Business Storytelling (Turning SQL results into strategic recommendations)

🎯 Business Objectives & Mission
The goal was to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights through four main pillars:

Sales Performance: Calculating Total Revenue, monthly trends, and peak sales periods.

Product Intelligence: Identifying Best Sellers vs. Low-performing items and analyzing Average Order Value (AOV).

Customer Profiling: Geographic distribution (Country/City) and simple RFM Segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary).

Strategy: Formulating data-driven recommendations to boost sales and optimize inventory.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Database: PostgreSQL (Advanced Querying & Data Cleaning)

Environment: VS Code

Methodology: Data Cleaning, EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis), and Business Metrics Calculation.

🗄️ Database Architecture
The analysis is based on 4 relational tables:

customers: Demographics and geographic locations.

sales: Transactional history (Order ID, Dates, Amounts).

products: Catalog details (Category, Unit Price, Stock levels).

payments: Payment methods used (Credit Card, PayPal, etc.).

🔍 Analytical Deep Dive
The project covers key retail metrics:

Top Revenue Products: Which items drive the most cash flow?

Monthly Revenue Growth: Identifying seasonal trends in the electronics market.

RFM Segmentation: Categorizing customers into "Champions", "At Risk", and "New" to tailor marketing efforts.

Payment Optimization: Analyzing preferred payment methods to reduce checkout friction.

💡 Strategic Business Insights
Based on the SQL analysis, this project provides recommendations such as:

Inventory Management: Phasing out low-cycle products to reduce storage costs.

Marketing Targets: Re-engaging "At Risk" customers identified through RFM analysis.

Geo-Expansion: Focusing ad spend on top-performing cities in the Francophone zone.
